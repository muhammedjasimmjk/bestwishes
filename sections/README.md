# Project Vision: Cinematic Birthday Journey

This project is an interactive, emotional, and premium digital birthday experience designed as a carefully crafted gift. It focuses on storytelling, intimate atmospheres, and progressive emotional beats.

---

## Design Language Guidelines

- **Aesthetic**: Dark luxury, minimal interface, glassmorphism, gold accents (`#D4AF37`), deep navy backdrops (`#08111F`).
- **Typography**: Cormorant Garamond for cinematic headings; Inter for clean, legible body text and actions.
- **Animations**: Soft, eased transforms, fades, and blur transitions. Avoid cartoonish, fast, or bouncing movements.
- **Tone**: Handcrafted, timeless, and deeply personal.

---

## Experience Flow

### Phase 1: Landing Hero (Completed)
- A cinematic introduction featuring the message: `"this is for and about you"`
- Subtle cursor-following radial gold backlight and floating canvas stardust.
- A premium, interactive glassmorphic button to begin.

### Phase 2: Voices of Love (Active Phase)
- A quiet transition into an intimate voice message space: `"Before we begin... There are a few people who wanted to say something."`
- Glassmorphic audio cards representing recordings from friends and family.
- Features: Name, profile photo, customizable audio controls, animated glowing sound waveform, and logic ensuring only one audio plays at a time.

### Phase 3: Moments
- A vertical storytelling gallery showing one photo and one warm personal memory text at a time.
- Smooth transition triggers as the visitor scrolls through the story.

### Phase 4: Things We Love About You
- A minimalist presentation of specific qualities (e.g., humor, stubbornness, kindness) that appear one by one with emotional, slow transforms.

### Phase 5: Birthday Celebration
- The emotional climax of the experience.
- The question: `"Ready?"`
- An interactive, premium birthday cake with candles that the user can blow out (via microphone or manual click).
- Climax: Elegant confetti, fireworks, and atmospheric effects.

### Phase 6: Final Letter
- A quiet, minimal ending featuring a full-screen, handwritten-style letter from the creator. No visual distractions.

---

## Core Principles

1. **Emotional Purpose**: Every visual and interaction must build the emotional journey.
2. **Restraint over Complexity**: Prioritize layout breathing room and typography over flashy visual effects.
3. **Clean Architecture**: Maintain clean, vanilla HTML/CSS/JS files to support seamless multi-phase integration.
